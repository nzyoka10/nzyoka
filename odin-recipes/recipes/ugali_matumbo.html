Ugali and Fried Matumbo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups maize flour (for Ugali)
  • Water
  • 1 kg matumbo (cleaned and chopped)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Ugali:

    2. Boil water in a pot. Gradually add maize flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.

    Keep stirring until the mixture thickens. Reduce heat, cover, and let it cook for a few more minutes.

    Once the Ugali is ready, let it cool a bit and then shape it into a ball or serve as preferred.

  2. Fry Matumbo:

    3. Season the chopped matumbo with salt and pepper.

    Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the matumbo and fry until golden brown.

    Ensure the matumbo is fully cooked. Remove from heat and let it drain on a paper towel.

  3. Serve:

    4. Place the Ugali and fried matumbo on a plate. Serve hot and enjoy!
