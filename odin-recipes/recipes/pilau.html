Pilau Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb (450g) meat (chicken), cubed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 cups beef or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice:

    2. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.

  2. Onions, garlic, and ginger:

    3. Heat oil in a large pot. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Saut√© until onions are soft and golden.

  3. Add meat:

    4. Add the cubed meat to the pot. Cook until browned on all sides.

  4. Add spices:

    5. Stir in the ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant.

  5. Add tomatoes:

    6. Add chopped tomatoes to the pot. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the mixture forms a thick paste.

  6. Add rice and broth:

    7. Add the washed rice to the pot and stir until well coated with the spice mixture. Pour in the beef or chicken broth and bring to a boil.

  7. Cook the pilau:

    8. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and let the pilau simmer until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.

  8. Fluff and serve:

    9. Once the pilau is cooked, fluff the rice with a fork. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious pilau!
