Githeri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed maize and beans (soaked overnight)
  • Water
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Vegetables, spices, or meat for additional flavor

Instructions:

  1. Soak the maize and beans:

    2. Rinse the mixed maize and beans and soak them in water overnight to soften.

  2. Cook the githeri:

    3. Drain the soaked maize and beans. In a large pot, add the soaked mixture and enough water to cover. Bring to a boil.

    Reduce heat to a simmer and cook until the maize and beans are tender. This may take 1-2 hours, depending on the type and size of the maize and beans.

  3. Add salt:

    4. Once the maize and beans are cooked, add salt to taste. Stir well and let it simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes.

  4. Optional additions:

    5. You can enhance the flavor by adding vegetables, spices, or meat. Some people like to add onions, tomatoes, or spices for extra taste.

  5. Serve:

    6. Once everything is cooked and seasoned to your liking, remove from heat. Serve the githeri hot and enjoy!
